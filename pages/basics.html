What is Breast Milk Donation?

Breast milk donation means sharing your extra breast milk with babies who need it most. Some mothers produce more milk than their baby needs, while some babies can‚Äôt get enough from their own mothers. Donating milk helps those little ones grow healthy and strong.

Why is it Important?

  • Breast milk gives babies natural immunity and strength.
  • It reduces the risk of infections and allergies in babies.
  • Helps save premature or sick babies who cannot get their mother‚Äôs milk.
  • Encourages kindness, sharing, and community support among mothers.

How to Donate Safely

1. Maintain Hygiene

Always wash your hands and use sterilized containers for collecting milk.

2. Store Properly

Keep the milk in clean, labeled containers and store it in the refrigerator or freezer.

3. Safe Delivery

Deliver the milk to the nearest milk bank or registered mother in a cold storage container.
